# Privacy Policy for PawPanic

**Last updated:** [INSERT DATE]

## Overview

PawPanic ("the App") is a free application that plays high-frequency sounds for pet entertainment. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data We Collect

### Data We DO NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Contact information
- Usage analytics
- Browsing history

### Data We Store Locally
- **Ad Consent Preference**: We store your choice regarding personalized ads on your device only. This is required for GDPR compliance and is never transmitted to our servers.

### Data Processed Temporarily
- **Photos and Videos**: When you capture your pet's reaction, the media is stored temporarily in the app's cache solely for sharing purposes. These files are automatically deleted and are never uploaded to any server.

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data for ad targeting

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can choose non-personalized ads through the consent dialog when first opening the app.

## Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| Camera | To capture photos/videos of pet reactions for sharing |
| Internet | To load advertisements |
| Network State | To check internet connectivity for ads |

## Children's Privacy

This app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:
- Access your data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase your data
- Object to processing
- Data portability

To exercise these rights regarding ad preferences, you can:
1. Clear the app's data in Android Settings
2. Reinstall the app to see the consent dialog again
