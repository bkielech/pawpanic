Privacy Policy

Overview

PawPanic ("the App") is a free application that plays high-frequency sounds for pet entertainment and reaction capture. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Summary: We don't collect or store your personal data. The app only saves your ad consent preference locally on your device.

Data We Collect

Data We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Usage analytics
  • Browsing history
  • Photos or videos you capture

Data We Store Locally

Ad Consent Preference: We store your choice regarding personalized ads on your device only. This is required for GDPR compliance and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data Processed Temporarily

Photos and Videos: When you capture your pet's reaction, the media is saved to your device's gallery. The app does not upload, store, or have access to these files after they are saved.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data for ad targeting

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

You can choose non-personalized ads through the consent dialog when first opening the app.

Permissions

Permission Purpose
Camera To capture photos/videos of pet reactions
Microphone To record audio in videos
Internet To load advertisements
Network State To check internet connectivity for ads

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights regarding ad preferences, you can:

  • Clear the app's data in Android Settings
  • Reinstall the app to see the consent dialog again

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data on our servers, there is minimal risk of data breach. Your ad consent preference is stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
